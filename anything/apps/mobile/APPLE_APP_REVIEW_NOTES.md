# Apple App Review Notes

## External Links Explanation

This app contains the following external links in the Learn tab. These links comply with Apple App Store guidelines for the following reasons:

### 1. Amazon Link — Planning My Escape by Dr. Mildred D. Muhammad

**Type:** Physical printed book purchase  
**Platform:** Amazon  
**Link:** https://www.amazon.com/Planning-My-Escape-Survivors-Domestic/dp/149527232X

**Compliance Note:** This is a PHYSICAL printed book available for purchase on Amazon. It is not a digital product and does not compete with Apple in-app purchases. The link allows users to purchase a physical, tangible book that will be shipped to their address.

### 2. Apple Books Link — Scared Silent Audiobook

**Type:** Digital audiobook  
**Platform:** Apple Books (Apple's own platform)  
**Link:** https://books.apple.com/us/audiobook/scared-silent-unabridged/id1877199612

**Compliance Note:** This is hosted directly on Apple's own platform Apple Books. This is not a third party digital purchase. This link drives users to Apple's own ecosystem and generates revenue for Apple.

### 3. Apple Podcasts Link — Rising Above It All Podcast

**Type:** Free podcast  
**Platform:** Apple Podcasts (Apple's own platform)  
**Link:** https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/rising-above-it-all-with-dr-mildred-muhammad/id1456293764

**Compliance Note:** This is a free podcast hosted on Apple's own platform Apple Podcasts. This link drives users to Apple's own ecosystem.

## In-App Purchases Compliance

All course content, subscriptions, and digital content within the app are purchased **exclusively through Apple in-app purchases via RevenueCat**. 

**No digital content is sold through any external links.**

The app implements a subscription model with the following tiers:
- **Free Tier:** Basic access to resources and safety features
- **Shield Tier:** Enhanced safety planning and check-in features
- **Rise Tier:** Full access to all 5 healing courses and premium content

All subscriptions are processed through Apple's in-app purchase system using RevenueCat as the subscription management platform.

---

## Privacy & Data Collection

The app collects and stores sensitive personal safety information with explicit user consent:

1. **Check-In Consent:** Users must consent before creating their first safety check-in, which stores details about incidents and safety concerns.

2. **Safety Plan Consent:** Users must consent before creating their safety plan, which stores personal safety information including emergency contacts, safe locations, and escape planning details.

Both consent dialogs clearly explain:
- What data is being stored
- That the data is encrypted and private
- That the data is never shared with anyone
- Where the data is stored (device + encrypted servers)

---

## User Disclaimers

All external links in the app include a clear disclaimer: **"Opens external website"** directly below the link button to ensure users know they are leaving the app.

---

## App Purpose & Mission

This app provides critical safety resources and healing support for domestic violence survivors. The external links serve to connect users with additional expert-created resources (books, podcasts) that complement the in-app safety tools and healing courses.

The app's primary monetization is through in-app subscriptions (processed via Apple IAP), not through any external link referrals.
